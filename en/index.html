To Use

Choose one or more collections to start. Use the filters to refine the searches and the visualizations menu to create different arrangements from the filtered works.

Filters

The filters are used to select which works will be displayed. They change the number of works included in the visualizations.

Visualizations

The visualizations present different ways to visually explore the works selected through the filters. Visualizations do not change the number of displayed works, only the order and positioning in which the works are arranged on the screen.

AI Curations

Selections made automatically by artificial intelligence, based on visual and semantic features of the artworks.

Export Images

Mosaics are images generated from the chosen filters and visualizations. Unlike the visualizations on screen, these images are not interactive, and their size varies according to the number of works included in the search. In the compositions, cropped objects are arranged according to their relative position and location in the original work.

Museums

Select one or more museums to see their works.
Use the buttons [all] and [none] to select or deselect all works at once.

Techniques

Use the buttons to show only the works that contain the selected techniques.
If none is selected, all works will be shown.

Dates

Use the sliders to limit the period of work that are shown.
