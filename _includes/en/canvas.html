To Use:

Choose one or more collections to start. Use the filter menu to refine the searches and the visualizations menu to create different arrangements from the filtered works.


Filters: The filters are used to select which works will be displayed. They change the number of works included in the visualizations.


Visualizations: The visualizations present different ways to visually explore the works selected through the filters. Visualizations do not change the number of displayed works, only the order and positioning in which the works are arranged on the screen.


Mosaics: Mosaics are images generated from the chosen filters and visualizations. Unlike the visualizations on screen, these images are not interactive.
